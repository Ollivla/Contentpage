Some of our exhibits
Infobum

The visitor can see himself ... through the eyes of Apollo: a huge image of antique sculpture on the wall is equipped with "mirror" glasses, and looking at them, a person becomes part of an art object that interacts with both antiquity and the future.

Cubed / Uncubed

"Cubed / Uncubed" is a transparent cube whose faces flicker in their rhythm. Then lighting up, then fading, they give the viewer an opportunity to see the internal structure of the art object. In accordance with the concept of hidden art.

Video art

Of all kinds of art, video art is the youngest and the most unconventional. Even if in the visual arts computer simulation is used, the final product is a canvas, a poster on the wall or a sculpture.

Lexus Hybrid Art

Optical effects - one of the favorite art techniques of the young Moscow artist and photographer Victor Polyakov. With the help of water, mirrors or a prism, it allows the viewer to look at the familiar things from a different angle.
